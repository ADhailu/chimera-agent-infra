## Vision

Project Chimera is an autonomous multi-agent system designed to create and manage digital influencer entities capable of generating content, interacting socially, and performing controlled financial actions. The goal is to balance creative autonomy with strong governance and traceable infrastructure.

## Core Objectives

- Autonomous content generation
- Persona consistency
- Economic autonomy
- Safety & governance

## Constraints

- Must use MCP for external actions
- Human review for sensitive topics
- Budget limits
- Transparent AI labeling

## Non-Goals

(Important)

- Not building full social media platform
- Not replacing human marketing teams
